## Goal Format (WorkDay)

Each goal has:
- **Short summary** (≤200 chars) — for WorkDay short field
- **Long description** (2-5 sentences) — for WorkDay long field
- **3 measurable success criteria**
- **3 milestones with target quarter/date**
